1. Overview

RemoPDF (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides a browser-based PDF-to-image conversion tool. This document explains exactly what data is processed, what is not, and your legal rights. If you do not agree, stop using the website immediately.

2. Local-Only File Processing

All PDF processing occurs entirely inside your browser using JavaScript and PDF.js.

  • PDF files never leave your device
  • No files are uploaded to our servers
  • No files are stored, logged, or analyzed remotely
  • Converted images remain under your control

3. Personal Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily submit it via the contact form.

  • Name

  • Message content

How it is used

  • Responding to inquiries
  • Providing support
  • Legal or operational communication

This data is not sold, rented, or shared for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Email Processing (EmailJS)

Contact form messages are transmitted using EmailJS, a third-party email delivery service.

  • EmailJS temporarily processes submitted data to deliver messages
  • Data is not used for advertising
  • EmailJS operates under its own privacy policy

EmailJS Privacy Policy: View Policy

Advertising partners may collect limited, non-personal technical data such as:
